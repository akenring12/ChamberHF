Chambers of Hope Foundation

Providing immediate relief and long-term support to refugees facing prolonged displacement. Our core project focuses on addressing the most urgent needs of refugees in camps—providing food, healthcare, clean water, and safety. In addition to these critical services, we aim to create sustainable pathways toward resettlement by offering educational programs, skills training, and advocacy.

About Us

The Chamber of Hope Foundation was founded out of a powerful calling received by Eldah Nasiani, whose vision was to make a lasting difference in the lives of refugees who have spent years, or even decades, stranded in camps. These individuals and families often endure unimaginable hardships, waiting for opportunities to rebuild their lives. The foundation was born from a deep desire to provide hope and a future for those who have been displaced by conflict, natural disasters, and political unrest.

Refugees often face daily challenges, including lack of access to basic resources such as food, healthcare, clean water, and safety. These struggles are compounded by prolonged waiting periods, leaving families in a state of uncertainty and despair. We seek to bring immediate relief to these individuals by addressing these critical needs while also helping them navigate pathways toward a more stable and secure future with dignity, safety, and hope.

Through strategic partnerships and the unwavering support of compassionate donors and volunteers, we create a tangible difference. With a focus on providing immediate aid, such as food and healthcare, by also working towards long-term solutions, helping refugees transition into welcoming communities and ultimately achieve resettlement.

Mission

The Foundation exists to bring immediate relief to refugees living in prolonged displacement by addressing their basic needs—food, healthcare, clean water, and safety. In addition, we are committed to creating pathways for long-term stability and resettlement, empowering refugees to rebuild their lives with dignity and self-sufficiency. Through our partnerships, dedication, and the generosity of our supporters, we work toward a world where every refugee has the opportunity to thrive.

Vision

Our vision is a world where refugees are not just provided for in the short term but are empowered to rebuild their lives in communities that welcome them with open arms. We believe that every refugee deserves a future filled with hope, opportunity, and the possibility of thriving in a safe and stable environment. Through collaboration, education, and support, we aspire to create a lasting impact on the lives of refugees around the world.

Refugee stories

Duressa Sakete: A Father’s Struggle for Family and a Better Future

Born in Ethiopia, Duressa Sakete’s life has been one of constant survival. Forced to flee government persecution, he arrived in Kakuma with his family, only to face new struggles—poverty, joblessness, and the devastating loss of his wife. Now a single father of five, he fights every day to provide for his children, dreaming of a life where they can escape the cycle of displacement.

Dominic Odwa: A Teacher’s Journey Through War and Displacement

In the heart of Kakuma Refugee Camp, amidst dust and uncertainty, lives Dominic Odwa—a man whose life has been shaped by war, loss, and an unbreakable dedication to education. Fleeing South Sudan after tragedy struck his family, Odwa found refuge in Kakuma. Despite the hardships, he chose to become a teacher, believing that knowledge could offer hope to children who had lost everything.

Aza Nsbimana: A Life of Struggle and Hope

Aza Nsbimana’s journey from Burundi to Kakuma was fueled by loss and hardship. Orphaned at a young age, she sought refuge in Kenya, hoping for stability. But life in the camp has been far from easy. As a mother, she battles poverty while striving to protect and heal her children from the trauma of violence. Despite the difficulties, Aza refuses to give up, holding onto the hope of a future beyond the camp’s borders.

How You Can Support

The Foundation relies on the generosity of individuals and organizations to continue its mission of providing critical relief and long-term support to refugees. There are several meaningful ways you can get involved and make a lasting impact:

Donate

Your financial contributions directly support our efforts to provide food, healthcare, clean water, safety, and other essential resources to refugees in need. Every donation, no matter the size, helps us bring immediate relief and create pathways toward stability and resettlement. You can donate one-time or set up a recurring donation to make a continuous impact.

Sponsor a refugee

By sponsoring a refugee or a family, you can make a significant difference in their journey to a better life. Sponsorship helps provide refugees with essential resources such as shelter, education, and healthcare, while also supporting their long-term resettlement efforts. Through sponsorship, you’ll have the opportunity to directly impact a refugee’s life and provide them with the tools they need to rebuild their future.

Volunteer

Volunteers are at the heart of the Foundation. Whether you're skilled in healthcare, education, project management, or fundraising, there are countless ways you can lend your time and expertise to support our mission. Volunteering can take place on the ground in refugee camps or virtually, and your efforts will help us provide the resources and support necessary for refugees to thrive.
